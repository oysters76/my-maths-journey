\[ \left( \frac{w^{-2}}{16v^{\frac{1}{2}}} \right) ^ \frac{1}{4} = \left( \frac{1}{16v^{\frac{1}{2}}w^{2}} \right) ^ \frac{1}{4} = \frac{1}{16^{\frac{1}{4}}v^{\frac{1}{8}}w^{\frac{1}{2}}} = \frac{1}{2v^{\frac{1}{8}}w^{\frac{1}{2}}} \]
